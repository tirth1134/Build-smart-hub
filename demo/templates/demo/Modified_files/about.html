About BuildSmartHub

A Crossplatform for Construction Professionals

This website is a medium between builders, contractors, raw material suppliers, architects, interior designers, electricians, plumbers, real estate brokers and common customers. Basically, our website is a platform for construction-related businesses and services. Through our platform, different construction businesses (small or large) get to showcase their products and services by posting and connecting to different business individuals. For example, if we look at the builder's perspective, the basic necessities for his business will be raw materials and various services. Now, our platform will give him a variety of options to choose from.

How It Works

From a business perspective, when a builder visits our website, he/she can access various contractors, raw material suppliers, architects, interior designers, electricians, plumbers, real estate brokers. It basically provides a platform for 2 kinds of people i.e. for businesses and customers.

From the customer's perspective, if a common man wants to build a house he/she can use our website to get different options for builders, architects, interior designers, etc. by looking at their work and ratings. Also for contacting on the spot a chatbot will be provided so that the direct contact of the business authorities and the customer can be made.
